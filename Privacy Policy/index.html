1. Introduction

Welcome to Fliptionary ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Fliptionary (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Game Progress: Your level completions, scores, and achievements (stored locally on your device)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App performance metrics, crash reports, and gameplay statistics
  • Advertising Data: Anonymous advertising identifiers for personalized ads (only with your consent)

2.3 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (only with your consent)
  • Expo/React Native: For app functionality and performance monitoring

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality

  • Display personalized advertisements (with your consent)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Process in-app purchases

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

4.1 With Your Consent

  • Advertising partners (Google AdMob) for personalized ads when you consent

4.2 Service Providers

  • Analytics services for app improvement

4.3 Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

  • Game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • This data is not transmitted to our servers unless explicitly stated

5.2 Security Measures

  • No sensitive personal information is collected by the app

6. Advertising and Cookies

6.1 Personalized Advertising

  • We use Google AdMob to display ads in the App

6.2 Non-Personalized Advertising

  • If you decline ad consent, you may still see non-personalized advertisements
